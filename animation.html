Animation

Between 2008-2016, Centore maintained an art practice that consisted mainly of the production of stop-motion animated shorts. Many of the satirical works that resulted are efforts to produce critical commentary on disparity and power by refocusing the visual language of Saturday morning cartoons. Through the incorporation of found materials and the use of claymation techniques that hearken back to classic animations, the works play with scale to posit new ways of experiencing everyday surroundings.

The Stop Motion Stomp Station

The Stop Motion
Stomp Station

The Stop Motion Stomp Station is a mobile workshop Centore developed to interactively teach the art of stop motion animation to the general public. It made appearances at Bartertown by artist Heather Hart at the DUMBO Arts Festival in 2012, 2013, and 2015, and Ladyfest Philadelphia in 2013.
